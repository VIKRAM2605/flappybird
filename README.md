# Flappy Bird — Shop Edition

A Flappy Bird clone built with **HTML5 Canvas**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript** — featuring an in-game shop, power-ups, and a currency system to keep things interesting.

---

## How to Play

| Control | Action |
|--------|--------|
| `Click` or `Spacebar` | Flap wings |
| Click skill slots (top-left) | Activate a power-up |

**Objective:** Fly through the pipes, rack up points, and use your power-ups wisely to survive as long as possible.

---

## Currency

Every point you score is worth **3 coins**. Spend them in the shop to stock up on power-ups before your next run.

---

## Shop

Open the shop from the main menu to browse and purchase power-ups using your earned coins.

---

## Power-ups

| Power-up | Effect |
|----------|--------|
|  **Shield** | Absorbs one hit and gives you a second chance |
|  **Rocket** | Destroys all incoming missiles |
|  **Invisibility** | Makes you invisible for 3 seconds — score multiplier ×5 during this time |
|  **Invincible** | Makes you invincible for 5 seconds |
|  **Gravity** | Slows down vertical movement for more control |

> Power-ups can be stacked for combined effects.
